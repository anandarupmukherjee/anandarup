About Me

Hello, I’m Anandarup (Anand). I currently work as a Research Associate at DIAL at the IfM, University of Cambridge, U.K. My main work areas deal with designing architectures, strategies, and solutions that look into COTS sensor-based data collection from industrial equipment. Holistically, I design and implement solutions for data collection and their communication under industrial settings for enabling Industrial IoT and Industry 4.0 architectures for SMEs. I completed my Ph.D. on UAVa/drones and their networks. My thesis looked into various aspects of enabling a fully autonomous and decentralized drone swarm capable of self-contained data processing and decision-making within the swarm itself, with no connection to any ground-based infrastructure whatsoever. Besides developing drone hardware, IoT solutions and add-ons for drones, and other IoT hardware, I got to use a lot of data-driven machine learning approaches for automation and decision making. As a result of my concurrent academic and entrepreneurial activities, I feel lucky enough to have been exposed to some of the relevant real-life and crucial challenges faced during the on-site deployment of IoT solutions, especially under the conditions of mobility and in various industrial settings. I am also a co-founder and Director for Sensordrops Networks Pvt. Ltd., my entrepreneurial venture with my Ph.D. supervisor and another one of my colleagues. What I do

Unmanned Aerial Vehicles

Sensing- predicting- recreating UAV controls, camera interfaces, networked streaming of UAV video and control feed, networked control of UAVs, autonomous aerial demarcation of ground plots, gesture-based controls.

UAV Networks

Network of UAVs, UAV-based ground crowd tracking, UAV-based mob tracking, data offload in UAV swarms, decentralized decision-making in networked UAVs, reinforcement-learning based processing offload in UAV swarms, sustainable UAV swarms.

Underwater Networks

Development of low-cost underwater transducers, physical layer for underwater communication, MAC layer for underwater communication, underwater multimedia streaming, underwater relay network, underwater surveillance.

Industrial IoT

Development of sensors for industrial fault monitoring in power stations, industrial mesh networks, Blockchains for Industrial IoT, Industrial IoT-based analytics and feedback.

Healthcare IoT

Development of portable healthcare monitoring systems, development of end-to-end mobile patient monitoring and recommender system, interoperable wearable healthcare units.

Agricultural IoT

Development of agricultural field monitoring systems, development of protocols for sustainable agricultural IoT, data-muling for remote agricultural zones.
